Conclusion

In conclusion, we have identified individuals who have experienced fraud or scam during online shopping. It is crucial for enhancing consumer protection and reducing financial losses.

Key indicators include unauthorized transactions, non-delivery of goods, and unusual account activity. Preventive measures such as secure payment gateways, multifactor authentication, and consumer education can mitigate risks. Collaborative efforts between e-commerce platforms, financial institutions, and cybersecurity agencies are essential to ensure a safer experience for consumers.
